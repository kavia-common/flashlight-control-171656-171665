Privacy Policy

This Flashlight app is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

No personal information is collected, tracked, or shared. The app operates entirely on-device.

Permissions

The app requests the Camera permission solely to control the device flashlight (torch). The app does not capture photos or video.

Network Access

The app does not require internet access for core functionality. Content displayed in these screens is bundled locally within the app.
